**Key findings from primary sources:**

1. **"Sub-genres of my life"** — Tarantino's phrase for martial arts films applies broadly. He treats sub-genres as identity-level commitments, not stylistic choices. This explains why he operates at high specificity (not "Western" but "Spaghetti Western").

2. **Genre invention** — When existing vocabulary fails, he coins terms. "A Southern" emerged in 2007 to describe Django's approach: spaghetti western aesthetics applied to antebellum slavery content, explicitly positioned against "big issue movies."

3. **The operatic triad** — Three non-negotiable elements: heightened genre storytelling + heightened music + operatic proportions. He describes even Pulp Fiction as "a rock and roll spaghetti western."

4. **Childhood revelation** — Abbott and Costello Meet Frankenstein taught him genre mixing before he had vocabulary for it ("I didn't know I was making genre distinctions when I was five, but I was"). He cites this as the origin of his entire career approach.

5. **Purist within mixing** — Film Comment captured this: "Tarantino achieves the remarkable feat of remaining a genre purist even as his films critique, embarrass, and crossbreed genre." Moral codes embedded in genres are preserved, not ironized.

6. **Genre as vehicle** — For Django, he explicitly states the strategy: "I want to do them like they're genre films, but they deal with everything that America has never dealt with because it's ashamed of it."

**Core insight extracted:** Genre conventions function as *implicit outlining*. Tarantino's "no outline" position is sustainable precisely because genre provides the structural endpoints that outlining normally supplies.

**The mechanism:**

```
GENRE_AS_OUTLINE:
  Kill Bill (revenge film)     → "I know I'm going to kill Bill"
  Inglourious Basterds (mission) → "The Basterds will complete their mission"
  Reservoir Dogs (heist)       → "The heist aftermath will play out"
```

The **what** (endpoint) is genre-determined. The **how** (navigation) is character-determined. Tarantino discovers the route while trusting the destination.

**Key primary source documentation:**
