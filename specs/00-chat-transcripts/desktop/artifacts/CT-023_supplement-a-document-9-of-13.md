## 4. Technical Requirements

### 4.1 Camera/Surveillance Grammar

**Primary Technical Challenge:** Multi-camera aesthetic.

**Requirements:**
- Multiple simultaneous camera angles
- Security camera aesthetic (fixed position, wide angle)
- Body cam aesthetic (movement, distortion)
- Phone/tablet cam aesthetic (vertical, intimate)
- Drone cam aesthetic (exterior delivery shot)

**Equipment Strategy:**
```
FIXED CAMERAS:
├── Security-style ceiling mounts throughout house
├── Over-fridge, air duct, mirror positions
└── Consider practical cameras visible in frame

MOBILE CAMERAS:
├── Body-mounted rigs for Li, Fre, Sus, Rok
├── Phone/tablet rigs for screen capture
└── Drone for exterior/delivery

POST REQUIREMENTS:
├── Quadrant compositing (opening)
├── CENSORED effect (consistent blur style)
├── Timecode overlay
├── Loop effect (frame repetition)
```

### 4.2 Smart Home Automation

**Requirements:**
- Doors that open/close on command
- Shutters that lower on command
- Temperature readouts (practical or VFX)
- Lighting changes per room
- Drug dispenser mechanism

**Implementation:**
- Practical automation where possible
- Safety protocols for door slam (Rok's foot)
- Consider split-screen for before/after house lockdown

### 4.3 Lef's Tank (VFX/Practical Hybrid)

**Primary Technical Challenge:** Gestating baby in tank.

**Requirements:**
- Floor-to-ceiling liquid tank (practical)
- Fetus/infant (VFX or practical puppet)
- Bubbling water effect (practical)
- "Boiling" escalation (practical bubbles + VFX enhancement)
- Blood from orifices (practical effects)
- Skin peeling (VFX or practical)

**Strategy:**
```
PRACTICAL ELEMENTS:
├── Tank construction (cyclorama tank)
├── Lighting within tank
├── Bubble machine escalation
├── Heating effect (lighting change + bubbles)
└── Blood release mechanism

VFX ELEMENTS:
├── Lef's body (full CG or practical puppet composited)
├── Body contortion/seizure (motion capture to CG)
├── Skin detail (enhancement)
└── Dream screen overlays
```

**Budget Note:** This is the most expensive single effect. Consider stylization to reduce cost.

### 4.4 Weather Effects

**Requirements:**
- Perpetual rain (all exteriors)
- Hail escalation (Act III)
- Interior rain sounds (throughout)

**Implementation:**
- Rain towers/rigs for practical exteriors
- Covered outdoor stage for controlled rain
- Post-rain enhancement as needed
- Sound design emphasis on rain atmosphere

## 5. Post-Production Considerations

### Picture

- Quadrant compositing (opening sequence)
- CENSORED effect consistency
- Timecode burn-in
- Color grade: desaturated, blue-gray palette
- Possible day-for-night for some exteriors

### Sound

- Layered surveillance ambience
- Distinct audio "character" per camera type
- Black Box voice design (multi-octave, choral)
- Siren/alarm design
- Rain/weather bed throughout
- Inaudible frequency effect (convulsion trigger)

### Music

- Classical music cue (Sus's morning)
- Possible score or sound-design-as-score approach
- Opening sync/rhythm could be entirely sound design
