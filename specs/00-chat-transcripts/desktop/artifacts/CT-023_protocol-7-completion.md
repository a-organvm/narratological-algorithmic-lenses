## Executive Summary

*Open View* (First Draft, 2018-03-29) is a formally ambitious surveillance thriller with genuine innovation in its multi-camera grammar and thematic density. The script demonstrates a writer with strong visual imagination and capacity for acidic domestic dialogue.

**Critical Strengths:**
- Opening quadrant sequence is genuinely inventive
- Bathroom argument achieves sharp domestic cruelty
- Li's hidden violence is effectively recontextualizing
- Thematic architecture (surveillance, family death, complicity) is sophisticated

**Critical Weaknesses:**
- Third act is incomplete (script terminates mid-climax)
- HQ subplot never integrates with family narrative
- Causal logic for Black Box activation is absent
- Multiple character arcs are abandoned

**Priority Actions:**
1. Complete third act (15-25 pages)
2. Decide HQ: eliminate or integrate
3. Establish "why this family" through causal logic
4. Center Li as protagonist
5. Resolve Sus/Mis Sar and Rok/Gre threads

**Verdict:** CONSIDER WITH MAJOR REVISIONS. The bones of a distinctive genre film are present. With a completed third act, clearer causal logic, and resolved subplots, this could be a compelling festival-track acquisition in the *Caché*/*Funny Games* lineage.
