## The Involuntary Response Hierarchy

**Core claim**: Horror and comedy are epistemologically privileged genres because they possess **binary physiological verification**—the body cannot lie about fear or laughter.

| Genre | Involuntary Marker | Verification | Failure Mode |
|-------|-------------------|--------------|--------------|
| Comedy | Laughter | Audible/visible | Silence |
| Horror | Fear response (startle, tension, dread) | Physiological | Boredom |
| Tragedy | Tears | Physiological but fakeable | Intellectual appreciation only |
| Drama | "Emotional engagement" | Subjective | Admiration without feeling |

Aristotle's catharsis and Bharata Muni's rasa theory both aim at involuntary states—but comedy and horror are the only genres where **the audience's body confirms or denies success in real-time**.
