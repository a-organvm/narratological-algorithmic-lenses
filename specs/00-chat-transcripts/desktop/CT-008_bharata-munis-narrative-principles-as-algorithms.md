## Narratologically Significant Gaps

Three areas warrant additional sections:

### 1. **Dhruva (Musical-Narrative Cues)** — High Priority
Songs that signal narrative beats: entrances, exits, emotional transitions, scene changes. These function as structural punctuation in the dramatic text.

### 2. **Special Representation (Viśeṣābhinaya)** — Medium Priority  
Algorithmic rules for representing:
- Death (8 stages from poison, disease, weapon)
- Seasons (visual/behavioral markers)
- Dreams and sleep states
- Speaking to invisible persons
- Aside/concealed speech
- Time of day (dawn, noon, dusk, night)

### 3. **Stage Zones (Kakṣyāvibhāga)** — Medium Priority
Spatial grammar: what stage areas signify (near/far, sacred/profane), movement patterns indicating location changes, the "east" convention.
