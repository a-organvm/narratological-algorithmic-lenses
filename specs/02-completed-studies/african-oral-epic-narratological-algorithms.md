# African Oral Epic: Narratological Algorithms

A systematic distillation by **Traditional African (Mwindo / Sundiata)** storytellers into formal, implementable principles for episodic narrative.

---

## 0. Meta-Principles (Axioms)

| Axiom | Formulation |
|-------|-------------|
| **AOE-A0** | Narrative is a **performative event**, not a fixed text; it lives in the interaction between the griot and the audience. |
| **AOE-A1** | Structure is **episodic and modular**, allowing for expansion or contraction based on context. |
| **AOE-A2** | Meaning is reinforced through **formulaic repetition and proverbs**, serving as structural anchors. |
| **AOE-A3** | The hero is a **vehicle for societal values**; their journey is the "rite of passage" for the entire community. |

---

## 1. Structural Hierarchy

The complete cycle is the **COMMUNAL_EPIC**. Each distinct narrative block is an **EPISODE**. The atomic units of storytelling are **FORMULAIC_PHRASES** (genealogies, proverbs, curses).
