# The Heroine's Journey: Narratological Algorithms

A systematic distillation of Maureen Murdock's *The Heroine's Journey: Woman's Quest for Wholeness* (1990) into formal, implementable principles for internal integration, psychological narrative, and circular structural progression.

---

## 0. Meta-Principles (Axioms)

| Axiom | Formulation |
|-------|-------------|
| **HJ-A0** | The journey is primarily **internal and psycho-spiritual**, focusing on integration rather than external conquest. |
| **HJ-A1** | Narrative progression follows a **circular or spiral path**, returning to reclaimed values with new understanding. |
| **HJ-A2** | Structural success is measured by the **harmonization of the feminine and masculine** aspects of the psyche. |
| **HJ-A3** | The central conflict is the **wounding of the feminine** and its subsequent healing. |
